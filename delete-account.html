Delete Your CanineNote Account

If you wish to permanently delete your CanineNote account and all associated data, you can do so directly in the app or by contacting us.

Delete your account in the app

Open CanineNote and navigate to:

More → Settings → Delete Account

Deleting your account permanently removes your account and associated data from our systems.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

caninenoteapp@outlook.com

Subject:

Account Deletion Request

Please send the request from the email address associated with your CanineNote account.

Data that will be deleted

  • User profile
  • Clients
  • Dogs
  • Appointments
  • Visit history
  • Treatment plans
  • Body maps
  • Reports
  • Uploaded business logo

Data retention

After successful account deletion, your personal data is permanently deleted unless we are required by law to retain certain information.
